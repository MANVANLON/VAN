A little about us

MAN & VAN As well as providing man and van services for individual heavy and bulky items we are also happy to undertake full house, flat and office removals. We have a fleet of vans so if you whether you need a single van and man to help you move flat or you need a large house removal requiring several helpers and even multiple vans just let us know! We have vans of different sizes as well from compact pick ups to Luton Vans capable of carrying up to one tonne. So no matter how big or small your removal job is we are confident that we can offer the right solution for you at the best price. We are happy to offer: Low price, fully insured vans Quick quotes Payment by cash or invoice The right van for the job, saving you money Clean vans equipped with sat-nav, trollies, blankets and straps Full loading and unloading services Disassembling and re-assembling of furniture No extra costs – all fuel and mileage included in costs Free seat for customers in the van if needed We can supply boxes and packaging materials Packing & unpacking services available
